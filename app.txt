This google maps web applicaiton provides markers for every library in Hamilton. On the left hand side users can filter the libraries by community and can choose to display all again.
Users can also find their location on the map by clicking the "My Location" button. Their location will then be marked with a marker resembling a person. The user also has the 
option to add their own marker by typing it under the address header and then clicking "Locate". The location will then be marked with a purple marker on the map. Lastly, the user can get directions to addresses they input. 
They have the choice to get directions based on driving or walking.
